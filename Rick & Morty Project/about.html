About

Inspiration of Rick and Morty


The show Rick and Morty started off as a short parody of Back to the Future. Justin Roiland came up with two characters, Doc and Marty, who eventually evolved into Rick and Morty. The Short was about a smart guy named Doc who bullied Marty, a not-so-bright guy, into doing things that Marty thought would fix his problems. It did not.



Creators

Justin Roiland


Justin Roiland is the voice actor for both Rick and Morty, as well as the main producer and writer. In the sound booth Roiland will sometimes record the banter between Rick and Morty as one take. Some scenes of the scenes shown are either improv or scripted before recording.



Dan Harmon


Dan Harmon procured an opportunity to produce and write a cartoon for Adult Swim, and inlisted Justin Roiland's help with character creation. Harmon had known about Roiland's back burner characters, Doc and Morty, for a while and decided to use them for development. Harmon's role in development is maintaining the story arc and narrative.
